A — Tab Switch (Edit / Preview)

Two tabs above the field. Click "Edit" to write markdown, "Preview" to see it rendered. Clean, familiar (like GitHub). Works great on mobile.

B — Side-by-Side Split Pane

Editor on the left, live preview on the right. Great on desktop. On mobile (390px) both panes would be very narrow and hard to use — not ideal for a mobile-first app.
